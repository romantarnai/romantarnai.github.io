History

Firstly in 1950's Subaru focused on making people's cars, which were small easily affordable vehicles. In 80's they've decided to go to motorsport. Their choice was rally.

At first they hadn't had much success, but then they've started shining. They had probably the most famous rally driver of all time Colin McRae and he used the famous impreza. It was packed with 4 door, all wheel drive, boxer_engine. Then they made turbocharged version named WRX and they've starded selling them in Japan.

In 1994 they introduced the STI verson of WRX, which was better faster version of WRX. In 1995 they have won wrc title and they went for headtrick. As celebration they introduced Impreza 22b, which was true rally car for street. They've started selling cars to whole world and in 2006 you could buy the hawk-eye version of WRX, by 2008 STI versions were made only in hatchback versions, which are sold to this day.

Boxer engine

Boxer engine's pistons move horizontally like boxer's fist. They are flat so they give car lowered center of gravity, so it has better stability.

Rally rivals

Rivality between Subaru Impreza and Mitsubishi Lancer EVO started in rally, mainly because they were rally giants, that no one could compare with.
